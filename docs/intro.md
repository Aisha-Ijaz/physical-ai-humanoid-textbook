# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook is designed for beginner-to-intermediate learners who want to understand the fundamentals of intelligent physical systems and humanoid robotics.

## About This Textbook

This textbook takes a practical application focus, with each chapter building upon previous concepts through progressive complexity. You'll find:

- Theoretical foundations of Physical AI and humanoid robotics
- Hands-on experiments (with both hardware and simulation options)
- Safety guidelines integrated throughout
- Visual learning aids and diagrams
- Knowledge checks to assess your understanding
- Ethical considerations in AI and robotics

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Undergraduate students in robotics, AI, or related fields
- Graduate students seeking to broaden their understanding
- Professionals transitioning into AI/robotics
- Enthusiasts with basic programming knowledge

## How to Use This Textbook

We recommend following the chapters sequentially, as each builds upon previous concepts. Complete the knowledge checks at the end of each section to validate your understanding before moving on. Try the hands-on experiments using either physical hardware or simulation tools (instructions provided for both).

Remember to always follow the safety guidelines when performing experiments.
